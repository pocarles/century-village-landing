Sound Familiar?

If you've ever served on a CV board, you know these headaches all too well.

"Did you get my email?"

You send notices. Owners say they never got them. Some prefer calls, others want texts, a few still want paper. Keeping everyone informed feels impossible.

"Are we even legal?"

Florida keeps changing COA rules. SB 4D, reserve studies, SIRS reports—you're a volunteer, not a lawyer. One mistake and your board could face serious liability.

"Where's that document?"

Meeting minutes from 2019? The roof warranty? That vendor contract? Everything's scattered across filing cabinets, email threads, and the previous treasurer's computer.
